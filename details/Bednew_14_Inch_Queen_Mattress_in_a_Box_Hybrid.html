14-inch Queen Mattress

Engineered to deliver the perfect blend of comfort and support, the Bednew 14-inch queen mattress is designed for a deeper, more restorative sleep. Its hybrid construction pairs high-density gel memory foam with individually pocketed springs to adapt to your body and maintain proper spinal alignment, reducing pressure points and ensuring even weight distribution.

Whether you're a light sleeper or sharing your bed with a partner, this mattress offers excellent motion isolation. That means you won't be disturbed by movements, so you can both sleep soundly and wake up refreshed. The breathable knitted fabric promotes airflow and regulates temperature, keeping you cool and comfortable all night long.
