Tatenale Ironing Mat with Gold Floral Design

This Tatenale ironing mat is designed with a multi-layer construction to ensure superior quality and long-lasting performance. The bottom layer features a silver-plated and quilted heat-resistant fabric, guaranteeing a secure and stable placement for your iron. You can use it on both sides, making it a versatile and durable solution for your home and travel needs.

Whether you're at home or on the go, this ironing mat is a must-have accessory.
